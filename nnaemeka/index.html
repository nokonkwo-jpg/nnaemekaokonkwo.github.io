About Me

Hello! My name is Nnaemeka, and I'm excited to share a bit about myself with you! I'm Nigerian-American and I speak Igbo, Spanish, and English. I have a younger sister who's also my best friend. I'm a sophomore at Trinity College pursing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a minor in Applied Mathematics.

When I'm not coding or in my studies, you'll often find me on the basketball court, (trying) to perfect my craft. I'm also love listening to podcasts, always eager to gain new perspectives. My favorites include: Philosophize This!, Stuff You Should Know, and Murder With My Husband. Additionally, I love taking refreshing nature walks, finding solace in the beauty of the great outdoors.
