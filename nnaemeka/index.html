I Am

Hello! My name is Nnaemeka, and I'm excited to share a bit about myself with you! I'm Nigerian-American and I speak Igbo, Spanish, and English. I'm a sophomore at Trinity College pursing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a minor in Applied Mathematics. Finally, I have a younger sister who's also my best friend.

When I'm not busy, I love to cook egwusi soup for my family, but eating is much more fun. Culture is a huge part of my identity and I like to stick close to that, watching Nollywood films and listening to Afrobeats. When I'm not doing any of the things above, I love to play basketball. Listening to podcasts is also a pastime of mine, My favorites being: Philosophize This!, Stuff You Should Know, and Murder With My Husband. Additionally, I love just talking to people. So please feel free to reach out and I look forward to getting to know you! :)
